# SaaS Revenue Churn Analysis
## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn, revenue trends, customer lifetime value (CLV), and customer acquisition costs (CAC) for a SaaS business. The objective was to identify churn drivers, evaluate customer profitability, and provide actionable business insights.

## Tools Used:
- SQL (MySQL)
- Power BI
- Excel

## Dataset Overview
The analysis was performed on:
- 600 customer subscription records
- 12 months of revenue and acquisition data

## Business Questions
- Which subscription plans experience the highest churn?
- Which acquisition channels bring the highest-risk customers?
- How does customer satisfaction (NPS) relate to churn?
- What is the estimated CLV for each plan?
- Which plans generate the strongest CLV ratios?
- How many customers are considered high-risk based on usage behavior?

## Key Insights
- Starter plan recorded the highest churn rate (71%).
- Referral and Partner channels showed the highest churn rates.
- Enterprise customers generated the highest estimated CLV.
- Customers who churned had significantly lower NPS scores than retained customers.
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR) showed a consistent upward trend throughout the year.
